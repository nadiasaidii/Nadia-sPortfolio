What is MBTI?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) test is an insightful psychometric tool that reveals your unique personality type. You can improve your decision-making, communication, and understanding of your own needs by increasing your awareness of yourself and those around you. And it's an asset to possess that ability. MBTI consists of 16 personalities.

INTJ

INTJ is a part of Analyst personality type. An architect (INTJ) is a person who is introverted, intuitive, thinking, and judging.

These astute strategists enjoy perfecting the details of life, bringing creativity and logic to everything they do.

Their inner world is frequently private and complex.
